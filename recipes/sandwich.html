Basic Sandwich Recipe

Description

This recipe consists of a flat surface of dough, with tomato sauce spread across.With mozzarella cheese over it and finally anchovies, pepperoni and mushrooms on top.

Put the mix on an oven at 200 °C for 15 minutes and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Anchovies
  • Misc. ingredients like onions, ham, pineapple, etc.

Steps

  1. Roll the dough and extend it over a circle
  2. Spread tomato sauce across the surface of the dough
  3. Put mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce
  4. Spread your other ingredients across the surface
  5. Put your pizza inside the oven at 200 °C for 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
